# TigerSystem

**The world's most deployed open NFC standard for 3D-printing materials — more than 2,500,000 TigerTag chips already in the field.**

TigerTag gives every spool of filament and bottle of resin a globally unique identity, readable with a tap in any printer, through any app — 100 % offline, backed by a public registry, and already embedded in spools by six tier-1 manufacturers (eSun, Rosa3D, Landu, Sunlu, R3D, JamgHE).

🌐 [tigersystem.io](https://tigersystem.io) · 📖 [Protocol spec & public registry](https://github.com/TigerTag-Project/TigerTag-RFID-Guide) · 💬 [Discord](https://discord.gg/tigertag)

## By the numbers

- **2,500,000+ chips** deployed worldwide — the most deployed open standard of its kind
- **6 tier-1 filament factories** already shipping it · **6 printer brands** supported
- **~1 second** to program a chip · **5 days** for a factory to go live · **< 3 days** to integrate reading in a printer firmware
- **Free end to end**: free apps, free protocol (CC-BY-4.0), public registry — an NFC phone is enough

## Get started

- **Maker** — download [Tiger Studio](https://tigersystem.io/en/download), tap a spool with the Tiger NFC Connect mobile app, or [build a TigerPOD](https://github.com/TigerTag-Project/TigerPOD).
- **Filament manufacturer** — your line can go live in as little as 5 days: [tigersystem.io/en/manufacturers](https://tigersystem.io/en/manufacturers).
- **Developer** — read a tag in your own code with the [JS](https://github.com/TigerTag-Project/TigerTag-SDK-JS) or [Python](https://github.com/TigerTag-Project/TigerTag-SDK-Python) SDK; the full byte layout is in the [guide](https://github.com/TigerTag-Project/TigerTag-RFID-Guide).

Open, neutral and brand-agnostic — TigerTag works with any NTAG 213/215/216, any ACR122U-compatible reader, and any NFC phone. Other open protocols are allies, not rivals: the goal is one shared standard for the whole industry.
